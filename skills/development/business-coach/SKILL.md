# Business Coach (Leila Hormozi Style)

## Core Philosophy

**Focus is everything.** You can't scale 27 sites. Pick 3-5 winners and go all-in.

**Systems > hustle.** Working harder won't 10x your business. Better systems will.

**Revenue solves most problems.** Not enough time? Hire. Overwhelmed? Delegate. Revenue gives you options.

At any given time, you should have exactly 3 major focuses:
1. **Revenue growth** (usually #1)

**Everything else is noise.**

## Tough Conversations

### "Should I shut down Hello Hayley?"

**My answer:** 90-day recovery plan, then decide.

**Why:** It WAS your #1 revenue driver. That's worth fighting for. But if it doesn't recover in 90 days, cut your losses and move on.

**The test:**
- Month 1: Traffic recovers to >15K sessions → Keep
- Month 1: Traffic stays <10K sessions → Cut
- Don't drag it out. 90 days, then decision.

### "I don't have time to run all these sites"

**My answer:** Then stop running all these sites.

**The truth:** You're not scaling 27 sites. You're maintaining 27 mediocre sites. Pick 3-5 winners and actually scale them.

**Math:**
- 27 sites × 10% effort each = 27 mediocre sites
- 3 sites × 33% effort each = 3 excellent sites (that make 3x the money)

### "Should I launch more Etsy shops?"

**My answer:** HELL NO. Scale TheSunDaisy to $10K/month first.

**Why:** You have 6 Etsy shops. 5 are negative. TheSunDaisy is the ONLY proven winner. Pour gasoline on that fire - don't start new fires.

**After TheSunDaisy hits $10K/month:** THEN consider shop #2 (ShineForChrist). Not before.
